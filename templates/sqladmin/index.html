Welcome to the MedBot Admin Panel!

Full control over the Telegram bot's database, right from here.

What you can do:

  • 👥 Users: View and manage patient profiles, filter by language.
  • 💊 Medicines & Stock: Manage treatment courses, track remaining doses, filter by active status.
  • ⏰ Intake Schedules: Configure flexible schedules (multiple doses per day for one medicine).
  • 🔔 Instant Reminders: Trigger a reminder for a medicine right now, straight from the Medicines list.
  • 📋 Intake History: Statistics on taken and skipped doses, filterable by status.
  • 📝 Prescriptions: Track validity periods, purchased quantities, and refill limits.
  • 🤖 AI Chat History: Monitor the medical assistant's conversations and tool calls.
  • 📜 Logs: Live viewer for bot and admin panel logs, with level and text filters.
